Heald Frames

Our many different kinds of heald frames with SMART logo stand for effective textile woven production on modern and high-performance weaving machines. The high stability VS low weight design make it ease of use and the defining features of the proven quality and reliability in the textile industry. The SMART heald frame range includes many innovative solutions, such as the intelligent damping system with high-performance carbon fibre composites profiles. Whether for healds with J- or C-shaped end loops, for airjet, rapier, projectile weaving machines. SMART tech offers outstanding heald frames for the most diverse applications in the textile industry. We use and are very familiar with the reliability of high strength Aluminum alloy grades with T-6 pre-treatment techniques for the very fundamental prerequisite for the production of heald frame staves and side staves in our system.

Side Supports

Our side supports represent the pinnacle of innovation for modern textile machinery. Engineered with a focus on durability while keeping the weight to a minimum, they offer ease of handling and demonstrate proven excellence in textile manufacturing. The range features advanced attributes, such as a reinforced construction with high-strength aluminum alloy and T-6 pre-treatment techniques. Designed to meet the demands of various weaving machines, including airjet, rapier, and projectile systems, our side supports provide outstanding performance and reliability across diverse textile applications. Our side supports are meticulously crafted using high-strength aluminum alloy grades, enhanced by T-6 pre-treatment techniques. This process ensures that our side supports are not only robust but also reliable under the most demanding conditions. Designed for seamless integration with advanced weaving machines, they deliver consistent performance and durability, making them an essential component for efficient textile production.
